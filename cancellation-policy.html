Cancellation Policy

Our flexible cancellation policy ensures you can learn German with confidence and peace of mind.

Our Cancellation Policy

At SebGabriel.com, we understand that life can be unpredictable. That's why we've designed a flexible cancellation policy that puts your needs first while ensuring our teachers can maintain their schedules.

We believe in transparency and fairness, so all cancellation terms are clearly outlined below. If you have any questions about our policy, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Individual Lessons

24+ Hours Notice

Cancel or reschedule your lesson with 24 hours or more notice at no charge. We'll work with you to find a new time that works.

Less than 24 Hours

If you cancel with less than 24 hours notice, you'll be charged 50% of the lesson fee. This helps compensate our teachers for the lost time.

No-Show

If you don't show up for your lesson without prior notice, you'll be charged the full lesson fee. Please contact us if you have an emergency.

Emergency Exceptions

We understand emergencies happen. Contact us immediately for illness, family emergencies, or technical issues, and we'll work with you.

Group Courses

1

Before Course Starts

Cancel your group course enrollment up to 7 days before the course begins for a full refund, minus any processing fees.

2

After Course Starts

If you need to leave a group course after it starts, you can receive a prorated refund for unused classes, minus a $25 processing fee.

3

Missed Classes

Individual missed group classes cannot be refunded, but you can access class recordings and materials to catch up.

Package Courses

5-Lesson Package

Cancel within 7 days of purchase for a full refund. After that, receive a prorated refund for unused lessons, minus a $25 processing fee.

10-Lesson Package

Cancel within 14 days of purchase for a full refund. After that, receive a prorated refund for unused lessons, minus a $25 processing fee.

Monthly Plans

Cancel your monthly plan at any time. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

Pausing Packages

You can pause your package for up to 30 days without losing your lessons. Contact us to arrange a pause.
