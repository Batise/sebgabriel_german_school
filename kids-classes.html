Kids German Classes

Fun, engaging German classes designed specifically for children ages 6-17. Learn German through games, songs, and interactive activities.

Why Choose Our Kids Classes?

Our kids German classes are designed by certified children's language teachers who understand how children learn best. We use age-appropriate methods, interactive games, songs, and stories to make German learning fun and effective.

Children naturally absorb languages more easily than adults, and our immersive approach helps them develop native-like pronunciation and natural language patterns from an early age.

Age-Appropriate Classes

Ages 6-8

Fun introduction to German through games, songs, and stories. Focus on basic vocabulary and simple phrases.

  • Colors, numbers, and animals
  • Simple greetings and introductions
  • German songs and nursery rhymes
  • Interactive games and activities

Ages 9-12

Structured learning with more complex vocabulary and basic grammar. Perfect for elementary school students.

  • Family, school, and hobbies vocabulary
  • Basic present tense
  • Simple conversations
  • German culture and traditions

Ages 13-17

Advanced German for teenagers. Focus on practical communication and preparation for academic or travel goals.

  • Complex grammar structures
  • Academic and social vocabulary
  • German literature and media
  • Exam preparation (if needed)
