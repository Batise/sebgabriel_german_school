About SebGabriel.com

Our Story

SebGabriel.com was founded by Sebastian Gabriel, a native German speaker with a passion for teaching and a vision to make German language learning accessible, effective, and enjoyable for students worldwide.

Growing up in Germany, Sebastian developed a deep appreciation for the German language and culture. After moving abroad and experiencing the challenges of learning new languages, he recognized the need for personalized, flexible learning solutions that could adapt to individual learning styles and goals.

What started as a personal mission to help friends and colleagues learn German has grown into a comprehensive platform that connects students with qualified native German teachers, providing high-quality education for learners of all ages and levels.
