Our Founder's Vision

Sebastian Gabriel

Founder & Director of Hallo Deutsch

Sebastian founded Hallo Deutsch with a clear vision: to connect passionate German learners with certified native teachers who understand the unique challenges of language acquisition. As the director, Sebastian oversees the organization of classes and coordinates trial sessions to ensure every student finds their perfect learning match.

Our carefully selected team of certified native German teachers brings together diverse specializations, ensuring that whether you're a complete beginner, a business professional, or a parent looking for kids' classes, you'll find the perfect teacher match for your specific needs.
