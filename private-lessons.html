Private German lessons offer the most personalized and effective way to learn German. With undivided attention from your native German teacher, you'll progress faster and develop confidence in speaking, listening, reading, and writing.

Whether you're a complete beginner or looking to perfect your advanced German skills, our private lessons are designed to meet you exactly where you are and take you where you want to go.

Course Levels

A1

Beginner

Learn basic German vocabulary, simple sentences, and essential grammar. Perfect for complete beginners or those with minimal German knowledge.

  • Basic greetings and introductions
  • Numbers, colors, and everyday objects
  • Simple present tense
  • Basic conversation skills
A2

Elementary

Build on your foundation with more complex grammar and expanded vocabulary. Start having real conversations in German.

  • Past and future tenses
  • More complex sentence structures
  • Everyday situations and topics
  • Basic reading and writing
B1

Intermediate

Develop fluency in everyday German. Handle most situations you'll encounter in German-speaking countries.

  • Complex grammar structures
  • Expanded vocabulary
  • Confident conversation skills
  • Reading German texts
B2

Upper Intermediate

Express yourself clearly and understand complex German texts. Handle professional and academic situations.

  • Advanced grammar mastery
  • Professional vocabulary
  • Complex discussions
  • German literature and media
C1

Advanced

Near-native fluency. Express yourself spontaneously and fluently without much obvious searching for expressions.

  • Nuanced language use
  • Complex professional communication
  • Cultural understanding
  • Academic German
C2

Proficiency

Mastery level German. Understand virtually everything and express yourself spontaneously with precision.

  • Native-like fluency
  • Complex professional writing
  • Cultural nuances
  • Translation and interpretation
