Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our German courses

What level should I choose?

We offer a free placement test to determine your exact level. If you're a complete beginner, start with our A1.1 Beginner Course. Our test will place you in the right level based on your current knowledge.

How long does it take to become fluent?

Fluency depends on your starting level, practice time, and learning goals. Most students see significant improvement within 3-6 months of regular study. Our structured approach helps you progress efficiently.

Do you offer courses for children?

Yes! We have specialized German classes for kids ages 5-17. Our children's courses use interactive, age-appropriate methods that make learning German fun and engaging for young learners.

Are private sessions truly one-on-one?

Absolutely! Private sessions are completely one-on-one with your dedicated teacher. You'll have the teacher's full attention and can customize the lesson to your specific needs and goals.

What's included in the course materials?

All digital materials are included at no extra cost: workbooks, exercises, practice materials, and audio files. You don't need to buy any additional textbooks or resources.

Can I reschedule or cancel lessons?

Yes! Private sessions can be rescheduled with 24+ hours notice for a full refund. Group courses have flexible policies. See our detailed Cancellation Policy for complete terms.

Do I need special software for online lessons?

No special software needed! We use standard video conferencing tools that work in your browser. Just need a stable internet connection, webcam, and microphone. We'll provide setup instructions.

What's the difference between group and private classes?

Group classes (max 12 students) offer peer interaction and are more affordable. Private classes give you personalized attention and flexible scheduling. Both include all materials and native German teachers.

How do I pay for courses?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. No hidden fees!

Can I try before I buy?

Yes! We offer a free placement test and trial options. Contact us to discuss the best way to experience our teaching before committing to a full course.

What if I'm not satisfied?

We offer satisfaction guarantees on our courses. If you're not happy after the first few lessons, we'll work with you to make it right or provide a refund.

Still have questions? We're here to help!
