About Hallo Deutsch

Your trusted partner in German language learning. We're passionate about making German accessible, enjoyable, and effective for students of all ages and backgrounds.

Our Story

Hallo Deutsch was born from a simple yet powerful belief: learning German should be fun, engaging, and accessible to everyone. Founded by Sebastian Gabriel, a native German speaker with years of teaching experience, our school has grown from a passion project into a trusted learning destination.

What started as one-on-one tutoring sessions has evolved into a comprehensive German learning platform, offering everything from beginner courses to advanced conversation practice. We've helped hundreds of students achieve their German language goals.

Today, we continue to innovate and improve our teaching methods, always keeping our students' success and enjoyment at the heart of everything we do.

Meet Sebastian Gabriel

Founder & Lead Teacher

Sebastian Gabriel is a native German speaker with over 8 years of experience in language education. Born and raised in Germany, Sebastian brings authentic German culture and language expertise to every lesson.

With a background in education and a passion for helping others succeed, Sebastian has developed innovative teaching methods that make German learning both effective and enjoyable. His approach combines traditional language fundamentals with modern, interactive techniques.

Sebastian has worked with students from over 30 countries, helping them achieve their German language goals for personal, academic, and professional purposes.

Fun Fact

Sebastian loves incorporating German culture, history, and everyday life into his lessons, making learning not just about language, but about understanding the German way of life!

Our Teaching Approach

1 Communication First

We prioritize real-world communication skills. From day one, students practice speaking and listening in practical situations they'll actually encounter.

2 Personalized Learning

Every student learns differently. We adapt our teaching methods to match your learning style, pace, and goals.

3 Cultural Integration

Language and culture go hand in hand. We integrate German culture, traditions, and social norms into our lessons naturally.

4 Progress Tracking

We monitor your progress closely and provide regular feedback to ensure you're always moving forward toward your goals.
