Group German Courses

Learn German in small, interactive groups with fellow students. Collaborative learning, peer support, and affordable pricing.

Why Choose Group Courses?

Group German courses offer the perfect balance of personalized attention and collaborative learning. With small class sizes (maximum 6 students), you'll benefit from peer interaction while still receiving individual feedback from your native German teacher.

Group courses are ideal for learners who enjoy social interaction, want to practice speaking with different people, and prefer the motivation that comes from learning alongside others.

What You Get

Small Groups

Maximum 6 students per class ensures everyone gets individual attention while benefiting from group dynamics and peer learning.

Interactive Learning

Engage in group discussions, role-plays, and collaborative activities that make learning German fun and memorable.

Affordable Pricing

Group courses offer excellent value for money, making quality German education accessible to more students.

Peer Support

Learn alongside fellow students who share your goals. Build friendships and support each other's learning journey.

Regular Schedule

Consistent weekly classes help you build a learning routine and maintain steady progress in your German studies.

Motivation

Group dynamics provide natural motivation and accountability. You'll stay committed to your learning goals.

Available Group Courses

A1

Beginner Group

Perfect for complete beginners. Learn basic German vocabulary, simple grammar, and essential conversation skills in a supportive group environment.

  • Basic greetings and introductions
  • Numbers, colors, and everyday objects
  • Simple present tense
  • Group conversation practice

8 weeks • 2 hours/week

A2

Elementary Group

Build on your foundation with more complex grammar and expanded vocabulary. Start having real conversations with your group members.

  • Past and future tenses
  • More complex sentence structures

  • Group reading and writing exercises

10 weeks • 2 hours/week

Intermediate Group
