Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our German language courses, pricing, and policies.

General Questions

What makes SebGabriel.com different?

We offer personalized German learning with native speakers, flexible scheduling, and a focus on practical communication skills. Our teachers are experienced professionals who adapt their teaching to your specific needs and goals.

Do I need any prior German knowledge?

No! We welcome complete beginners. Our teachers will assess your level during the trial lesson and create a personalized learning plan that starts exactly where you are.

How quickly will I learn German?

Learning speed varies by individual, but most students see significant progress within 3-6 months of regular lessons. With our personalized approach, you'll typically progress faster than in traditional group classes.

Can I learn German for specific purposes?

Absolutely! We offer specialized courses for business German, travel, academic study, exam preparation, and more. Just let us know your specific goals during your trial lesson.

Course Questions

What's included in the trial lesson?

The 30-minute trial lesson includes a level assessment, discussion of your goals, and a sample lesson to experience our teaching method. The $10 fee is credited toward your first course.

How long are the regular lessons?

Private lessons are typically 60 minutes, group courses are 60-90 minutes, and kids classes are 30-60 minutes depending on age. We can adjust lesson length based on your preferences.

What materials do I need?

We provide all learning materials digitally. You'll need a computer or tablet with internet connection, microphone, and camera for online lessons. No additional textbooks required!

Can I change teachers if needed?

Yes! We encourage you to try different teachers to find the best match for your learning style. You can request a different teacher at any time.

Scheduling & Flexibility

How flexible is the scheduling?

Very flexible! You can schedule lessons at times that work for you, including evenings and weekends. We accommodate different time zones and can adjust schedules as needed.

What if I need to cancel a lesson?

You can cancel or reschedule lessons up to 24 hours in advance at no charge. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a fee. We understand that life happens!

Can I pause my course?

Yes, you can pause your course for up to 30 days. For longer breaks, we can discuss options to maintain your progress and learning momentum.

Do you offer weekend classes?

Absolutely! We offer classes seven days a week, including weekends. Many of our students prefer weekend lessons to fit their busy schedules.

Pricing & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely through Stripe with SSL encryption.

Are there any hidden fees?

No hidden fees! The price you see is what you pay. All learning materials and support are included in the course price.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee for your first lesson. For package courses, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis with prorated amounts.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my package?

Yes! You can change your package at any time. We'll prorate any differences in pricing to ensure you get fair value.

Technical Requirements

What technical requirements do I need?

You need a computer, tablet, or smartphone with internet connection, microphone, and camera. We recommend a stable internet connection for the best experience.

What platform do you use for online lessons?

We use Zoom for online lessons, which works on all devices and browsers. We'll send you a link before each lesson - no software installation required!

What if I have technical issues?

Our support team is here to help! We provide technical support before and during lessons to ensure everything runs smoothly.

Can I record my lessons?

Yes, you can record your lessons for review purposes. We'll provide you with the recording after each lesson to help you practice.

Kids Classes

What age groups do you teach?

We teach children ages 6-17, with age-appropriate groups: 6-8, 9-12, and 13-17. Each group uses different teaching methods and materials.

Are your kids teachers certified?

Yes! All our kids teachers are certified in child education and have experience teaching German to children. They use fun, interactive methods.

Can parents observe the lessons?

Parents are welcome to observe lessons, especially for younger children. We also provide regular progress reports to keep parents informed.

Do you offer family classes?

Yes! We offer family classes where parents and children learn German together. It's a great way to practice as a family.

Business German

Do you offer corporate training?

Yes! We provide corporate German training for teams and departments. We can customize programs for your specific industry and needs.

Can you help with German business etiquette?

Absolutely! Our business German courses include cultural training, business etiquette, and professional communication skills.

Do you prepare students for German exams?

Yes! We offer exam preparation for Goethe, TestDaF, DSH, and other German language exams with targeted practice and strategies.

Can you help with German job interviews?

Yes! We provide specialized training for German job interviews, including common questions, professional vocabulary, and cultural tips.
